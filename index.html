About Cafe


Since 2018

The Cafe was founded by Mr. Khairul Mizan and partners in 2018 and were located in Kota Kemuning. The concpet of this cafe is western aesthetic inspired by the late night scenary of Paris in the mid of july. you can enjoy tasty meals with a peaceful scenary whereby the cafe was surrounded with light of concrete city. The design of the bilding is concrete jungle where we decor the cafe with small indoors plants

Our cafe is also open to all customers. Customers especially teenagers definitely choose this cafe as their "favorite spot" to fill their free time or "hangout" with their friends, have fun with them and have the conversations after a long time they have not met.
